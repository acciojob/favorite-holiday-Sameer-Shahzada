Dubai


Dubai is a vibrant city in the United Arab Emirates known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and desert adventures. It offers a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

  1. Burj Khalifa – The tallest building in the world.
  2. Dubai Mall – One of the largest shopping malls globally.
  3. Palm Jumeirah – A stunning man-made island.
  4. Dubai Marina – A beautiful waterfront destination.
  5. Desert Safari – An unforgettable adventure experience.
  • Shawarma – A popular Middle Eastern street food.
  • Al Harees – A traditional Emirati dish.
  • Machboos – A flavorful rice and meat dish.
  • Luqaimat – Sweet dumplings served with syrup.
  • Grilled Kebabs – A must-try delicacy.
